Jesus Quiroz

I hold a Biology B.S degree from UC Santa Cruz where I researched molecular mechanisms of neurodegeneration and synaptic plasticity using a Drosophila Alzhiemer's disease model in Dr. Jeremy Lee's Lab

My work spans characterizing rational Aβ42 mutations, in vivo lifespan assays, and psychoplastogens that modulate synaptic function. I’m applying to PhD programs in the molecular life sciences to develop the scientifc scaffolding---mechanistic thinking, experimental design, approaches---to develop objectives & aims towards any facet of molecular biology research.
